Delete Account & Data

Cubiqo does not require an account. All data is stored locally on your device.

What data does Cubiqo store?

Cubiqo stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • High score (per game mode)
  • Daily challenge streak and last played date
  • Settings (sound, vibration, theme)

No data is stored on any external server.

How to delete your data on Android

  1. Open Settings on your device
  2. Go to Apps → Cubiqo
  3. Tap Storage → Clear Data
  4. All local game data will be removed

Alternatively, uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

How to delete your data on iOS

  1. Delete the Cubiqo app from your device
  2. All local game data will be automatically removed
