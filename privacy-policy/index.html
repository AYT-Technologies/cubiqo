Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

AYT Technologies ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Cubiqo mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Cubiqo does not directly collect any personal information. The app stores your game progress (high score, daily challenge streak) locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob - We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics - We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns. Firebase may collect device information and usage data. For more information, see Firebase Privacy.

Data Storage

All game data (high score, daily streak) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store any personal data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Cubiqo is suitable for all ages. We comply with COPPA regulations and configure our ad services for child-directed treatment. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Advertising

We display the following types of ads:

  • Banner ads - Displayed at the bottom of the game screen
  • Interstitial ads - Displayed between games (every 3 games)
  • Rewarded ads - Optional video ads that allow you to continue playing after game over

All ads are configured with child-directed settings and limited to age-appropriate content (rated G). You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

Permissions

Cubiqo requires the following permissions:

  • Internet - Required to load and display advertisements
  • Network State - Required to check connectivity for ad loading

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
