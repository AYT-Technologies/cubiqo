Support & FAQ

How do I play Cubiqo?

Drag and drop block pieces onto the 10x10 grid. Fill complete rows or columns to clear them and earn points. The game ends when you cannot place any more pieces.

What are the game modes?

Cubiqo has three modes: Classic (standard play), Turbo (wild pieces with special blocks), and Rush (race against the clock). There is also a Daily Challenge — one puzzle per day with streak tracking.

How does the combo system work?

Clear multiple lines in quick succession to build combos. Each combo increases your score multiplier, so try to set up chain clears for maximum points!

What does the "Continue" option do?

When the game is over, you can watch a short video ad to continue playing. This clears the bottom rows of the board to give you more space.

How does the Daily Challenge streak work?

Reach the daily target score each day to keep your streak alive. Missing a day resets your streak back to zero.

Is my high score saved?

Yes! Your high score and daily streak are automatically saved on your device.

The app is crashing or not working properly

Try these steps: 1) Close and reopen the app. 2) Restart your device. 3) Make sure you have the latest version installed. If the issue persists, contact us.
